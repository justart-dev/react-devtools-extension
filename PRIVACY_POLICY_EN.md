# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2025**

Taillog ("the Extension") values your privacy and complies with applicable regulations. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension collects and how it is used.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information
The Extension processes the following information **only within your browser**:

- **Console Logs**: Log messages from console.log, console.error, etc. on web pages
- **Network Requests**: HTTP request/response information via fetch and XMLHttpRequest
- **Clipboard History**: Text copied by the user (when using the Stash feature)

### 1.2 Information We Do Not Collect
The Extension does **NOT** collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Login credentials or passwords
- Payment information
- Location data
- Cookies or tracking data

---

## 2. Data Storage and Processing

### 2.1 Local Storage
All data is stored **only on your device**. The Extension uses Chrome's `storage.local` API to store clipboard history locally.

### 2.2 No External Transmission
The Extension does not transmit any data to external servers. All processing occurs within your browser.

### 2.3 Data Retention
- Console logs and network requests: Retained only during the browser session (maximum 100 entries)
- Clipboard history: Retained until deleted by the user or the Extension is removed (maximum 10 entries)

---

## 3. Information Sharing

The Extension does not share any collected information with third parties. Data exists only on your device and is not transmitted externally.

---

## 4. Your Rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time:

- **Delete Data**: You can delete stored clipboard history via the "Clear" button in the Stash tab.
- **Remove Extension**: Removing the Extension will delete all associated data.

---

## 5. Security

The Extension takes the following measures to protect your data:

- All data is stored only on your local device.
- No communication with external servers eliminates the risk of data leakage.
- Sensitive information (passwords, authentication tokens, etc.) is not intentionally collected or stored.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 14 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Significant changes will be communicated through Extension updates.
